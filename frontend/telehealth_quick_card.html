VA Telehealth Quick Card

📹What is VA Telehealth?

  • See your VA doctor, nurse, or therapist on a phone, tablet, or computer
  • Talk from your shelter, housing, clinic, or other safe place
  • Save time, skip travel, and still get the care you need

⏰Why Should I Try It?

  • ✓ No travel or waiting rooms
  • ✓ Easy access to primary care, mental health, medications
  • ✓ A health care navigator or VA staff can help set it up

📱What Do I Need?

  • Smartphone, tablet, or computer
  • Internet or Wi-Fi connection (ask about free VA Wi-Fi or local hotspots)
  • VA Video Connect app (navigator can help install it)
  • BUT - if you don’t have a device or internet, ask about the VA Digital Divide Program.
    • You may qualify for a loaner tablet with internet access.
    • Your Health Care Navigator, VA staff or social worker can help you apply.

📝How Do I Start?

  1. Ask your health care navigator or VA primary care team for a telehealth appointment
  2. Check your phone or email for the appointment link
  3. At the time of the visit, click the link and join the call

❓Need Help?

  • Consider a test visit with your SSVF Health Care Navigator
  • Ask your navigator or VA social worker
  • Call VA Telehealth Help Desk → 1-866-651-3180
  • Visit www.telehealth.va.gov
